## 🚀 Quick Setup Process (Google Workspace)

### Step 1: Purchase Google Workspace
1. **Go to**: workspace.google.com
2. **Choose**: Business Starter ($6/month)  
3. **Domain**: Enter ifsexpert.ai
4. **User**: Create xcey@ifsexpert.ai account

### Step 2: Verify Domain Ownership
- **Google provides**: DNS verification record
- **Add to Namecheap**: One additional DNS record
- **Verification**: Usually within 24 hours

### Step 3: Configure Email Settings
- **MX Records**: Point email to Google servers (Google provides exact records)
- **Add to Namecheap**: Same Advanced DNS section where you added A records
- **Result**: Professional email working in 24-48 hours
