Pricing FAQ

How do you determine project pricing?

Based on project complexity, scope, timeline, and customization requirements. Every project gets a detailed estimate after initial assessment.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Most projects are split into milestone-based payments (typically 25% upfront, 50% at key milestones, 25% at completion).

What if my project scope changes?

Scope changes are handled transparently with written approval before any additional work begins. You always know the cost impact upfront.

Do you work with US companies?

Absolutely. We work with manufacturers across North America. Pricing is in CAD but we're happy to provide USD estimates.

What's your cancellation policy?

Hourly work can be cancelled anytime. Project work requires 30-day notice. You only pay for work completed to that point.
