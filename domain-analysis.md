# Domain Strategy Analysis: xceyifsconsulting.com vs ifsconfig.ai

## Current Option: xceyifsconsulting.com
### ✅ Pros
- **Personal Branding**: Builds trust around xcey as the expert
- **SEO Established**: Already indexed by search engines
- **Professional**: Clear indication it's a consulting service
- **Descriptive**: Immediately tells visitors what the business does
- **Business Continuity**: All current links, emails, and materials work

### ❌ Cons  
- **Long URL**: Harder to type and remember
- **Personal Dependency**: Business tied to xcey's personal brand
- **Less Scalable**: Harder to expand beyond xcey as sole consultant

---

## Proposed Option: ifsconfig.ai
### ✅ Pros
- **Memorable**: Short, catchy, easy to type
- **Modern Positioning**: .ai extension suggests tech-forward approach
- **Scalable Brand**: Not tied to individual person
- **Niche Authority**: "config" suggests IFS configuration expertise
- **Marketing Edge**: Could differentiate from traditional consultants
- **Premium Feel**: .ai domains have tech startup credibility

### ❌ Cons
- **Domain Cost**: .ai domains are expensive ($80-200/year vs $12/year for .com)
- **SEO Reset**: Start from zero with search engine rankings
- **Rebrand Cost**: Need to update all materials, business cards, email signatures
- **Scope Limitation**: "config" might seem narrower than "consulting"
- **Link Breakage**: All existing business card/email references become invalid
- **Brand Recognition Loss**: Start over building brand awareness
